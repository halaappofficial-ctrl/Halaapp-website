Delete your account

This page explains how to delete your HALA MOVE account and exactly what happens to your data. It applies to both the HALA Move (customer) app and the HALA Move Driver app.

1Delete from inside the appFastest

  1. Open the HALA Move app (or HALA Move Driver).
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
Settings › Delete Account

Your account and associated personal data are permanently removed. This cannot be undone.

2If you can no longer open the app

Email contact@halamove.co.za from the email address (or quoting the phone number) linked to your account, with the subject “Delete my account”. We verify your identity and then delete the account for you. You can also reach us on WhatsApp +27 65 891 5709.

3What we delete

When your account is deleted we permanently remove your profile and account details (name, phone number, email address), your saved addresses, your job and booking history, item and delivery photos you uploaded, in-app chat messages and ratings — and, for drivers, your vehicle details, identity-verification documents (ID and selfie) and bank payout details.

4What we may keep, and why

We retain only what the law requires — for example transaction and payment records needed for financial, tax and regulatory compliance, and any records needed to resolve an open dispute or meet a legal obligation. These are kept only as long as required and are not used for anything else. GPS location history, delivery and pickup photos, and in-app chat are in any case automatically deleted 90 days after a job is completed.

5How long it takes

Your access ends immediately when you delete your account. Full removal of associated personal data from our systems is completed within 30 days of your request.
