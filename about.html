About HALA

Built to replace a system that never worked.

HALA is a technology platform that connects South Africans who need to move large items with verified drivers who bid for the job in real time. This is why it exists.

The Problem We Solve

You have been there. You need to move a couch, a washing machine, a whole flat — and you start making calls. Someone in a WhatsApp group knows a guy. The guy says he’ll be there at 10. He arrives at 2. Or he doesn’t arrive at all. You phone again. He asks for more than he quoted. You have no choice because the truck is already booked and the deposit is paid on the new place. You load your things. Something gets scratched. He says it was already scratched. You have no proof. He leaves with the money. You are left with the damage and no recourse.

This is not a bad luck story. This is the normal experience of moving anything large in South Africa. And it is not the driver’s fault alone — it is the absence of any system. No verification. No tracking. No documentation. No accountability. Just a phone number and a prayer.

Business owners have a different version of the same problem. You need regular deliveries — materials to a construction site, stock to retail branches, supplies to a medical facility. You arrange a driver. He does not show up one morning and you find out via your client, whose delivery never arrived. You have no record, no proof, and no way to show that you took reasonable steps to ensure the delivery happened. You absorb the cost and the relationship damage.

HALA was built to end this. Not by replacing drivers — by giving them a system that makes them more trustworthy, more accountable, and more consistently employed. And by giving customers the verification, the tracking, and the documentation that makes handing their belongings to a driver feel safe.

How HALA Works

You post your job. You describe what you need to move, where it’s being collected, where it’s going, and what floor you’re on. Our AI calculates a fair price range based on the details you’ve entered, before any driver has seen your job.

Nearby verified drivers see your job and bid. You see each driver’s name, photo, vehicle, star rating, and completed job count alongside their bid. You choose the best combination of price and quality. Your payment is held securely in escrow — it does not move until the job is done.

Your driver approaches and you watch them on the live map. Before they load a single item, they photograph everything. You review and approve. The driver navigates to your dropoff. More photos at delivery. You confirm. Payment releases to the driver. Both of you rate the experience. Done.

The whole process — from posting to confirmation — runs inside one app. No calls. No cash. No uncertainty about who is coming or whether they have arrived. Everything is documented and every step is timestamped.

Our Values

Verified, not just verified-looking

Every driver on HALA has their South African ID checked against the government database before they see a single job. Their vehicle is photographed and their roadworthy certificate is on file. When HALA tells you a driver is verified, it means something specific — not a checkbox ticked, not a form self-filled. A real process with a real outcome. If a driver’s roadworthy expires, their account pauses automatically until they upload a current certificate.

Accountable from pickup to dropoff

Timestamped photos at every pickup and delivery. GPS trail that records every route. In-app status updates at each stage that cannot be backdated. Customer approval of item condition before a single item is touched. This is the HALA photo protocol — and it is mandatory, not optional. It protects customers against damage claims. It protects drivers against false ones. When a dispute arises, there is evidence. The evidence tells the story. The outcome is fair.

Fair to the people who do the work

Drivers keep 80% of every rand they earn. Not 60%, not 70% — 80%. Payment lands in their account the same day the job is completed, for jobs done before 5pm. There is no monthly subscription fee to be on the platform. No registration cost. No minimum number of jobs. A driver who joins HALA and does one job in their first week has nothing to lose and everything to gain. We built it this way because a platform that treats its drivers fairly is a platform that attracts the best drivers — and the best drivers are what customers need.

Built for South Africa, growing across Africa

HALA launched in Durban. Not as a pilot, not as a test — as a fully committed launch. KwaZulu-Natal is one of the most logistically active provinces in South Africa and one of the most underserved by reliable verified transport. We are growing from here to Johannesburg and Cape Town, and from there across the continent. Every city we enter will have the same experience: verified drivers, live tracking, same-day payment. The standard does not change as we grow.

Our Commitment to Drivers

HALA was built to give South African drivers ownership of their income. Not the word “ownership” in a motivational sense — actual ownership of the mechanism. When you are on HALA, your earnings are a function of what you bid, how many jobs you accept, and how well you do the work. Nobody can change the formula without telling you. Nobody can lower your cut without your knowledge. The 80/20 split is documented, permanent, and consistent.

Same-day payment is not a feature we added to the list. It is the foundation. A driver who knows their money lands today — not Friday, not at month-end, but today — builds their life differently. They plan differently. They invest back into their vehicle. They show up for every job because every job has immediate, tangible value. This is not a small thing. For many of our drivers, the shift from a weekly or monthly payment cycle to a same-day cycle is the most significant financial change in their working life. We treat it with the dignity it deserves.
