How to install on Android

Since HALA MOVE is in pioneer release, you'll install the APK directly. The process takes about a minute.

STEP 1

Tap Download above

The green or blue button downloads the HALA MOVE APK straight to your phone. Look for the file in your notification bar or Downloads folder.

STEP 2

Allow installs from your browser

If prompted, go to Settings > Apps > Special access > Install unknown apps. Allow your browser (Chrome, Brave, etc.).

STEP 3

Open the downloaded file

Tap the file in your notification bar or Downloads folder. Confirm "Install" when Android asks.

STEP 4

Sign up & go

Open HALA MOVE, enter your phone number, verify the OTP, and you're in. Drivers complete the quick verification wizard.

Safe to install. HALA MOVE is built and signed by PROFITSAGE PTY LTD (CIPC-registered, DUNS 366869273). The pioneer APK is the same code being submitted to Google Play. If your Play Protect shows a warning, tap "Install anyway" — this is standard for pre-store apps.
