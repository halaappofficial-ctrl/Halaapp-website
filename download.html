How to install on Android

HALA MOVE is on Google Play. Two taps and you're in — no unknown-sources settings, no downloaded files, and updates arrive on their own.

STEP 1

Tap the Google Play button above

Pick Driver if you own a bakkie and want to earn, or Customer if you need something moved. It opens the app straight in the Play Store.

STEP 2

Tap Install

Google Play handles the rest. If you installed an earlier APK of HALA MOVE, uninstall that one first.

STEP 3

Sign up & go

Open HALA MOVE, enter your phone number, verify the OTP, and you're in. Drivers complete the quick verification wizard.
